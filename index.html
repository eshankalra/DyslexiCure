About

Dyslexia is a learning disorder that affects reading, writing, and spelling abilities. It is estimated that around 10-20% of the world's population is affected by dyslexia. A website dedicated to helping dyslexia patients can be beneficial in many ways. "DyslexiCure " is a website dedicated to helping dyslexia patients could provide various resources, such as information on dyslexia, strategies for managing dyslexia, and tools for improving reading and writing skills
